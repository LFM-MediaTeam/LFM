About the ministry
Living Faith Ministries PRC (People's Restored Church) is a vibrant faith community dedicated to spreading hope, love, and the teachings of Jesus Christ. Based in Pretoria, we gather online and in-person to worship, uplift, and serve.

Our mission

To equip believers, feed the hungry, and bring spiritual restoration through the Word. We share daily devotionals, live sermons, and community outreach on our Facebook page @LivingFaithMinistries-PRC.
